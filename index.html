Creamy Tomato Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pasta (penne, spaghetti, or your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to package directions until al dente. Drain and reserve 1/2 cup of the pasta water.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and bring to a simmer. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Stir in the heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the sauce is heated through and slightly thickened.
  5. Add the cooked pasta and reserved pasta water to the sauce. Toss gently to coat the pasta evenly.
  6. Serve immediately, garnished with fresh basil leaves if desired.
